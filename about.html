The ALCU Games (Association of Local Colleges and Universities Games) is a yearly sports event for students from local colleges and universities in the Philippines.




The goal of the ALCU Games is to bring students together through sports like volleyball, basketball, athletics, and more. It teaches sportsmanship, friendship, teamwork, and discipline.




The ALCU Games 2025 was full of exciting games, teamwork, and fun experiences. Athletes from different schools joined to show their talents and represent their schools with pride.It was not just about winning, but also about building confidence, learning from others, and making new friends.
